About This Data
Data Source: CMS Medicare Physician & Other Practitioners dataset via data.cms.gov public API (routed through a CORS proxy for local file access).

What it contains: Services, procedures, utilization counts, and Medicare payment amounts per provider, organized by NPI and HCPCS/CPT code.

Use case: Identify high-volume surgeons and providers who perform specific procedures — ideal for medical device sales targeting.

Tip: For best results, search by a specific HCPCS code (e.g. 27447 for total knee replacement) plus a state to find top-volume surgeons in a territory.

Note: Data reflects Medicare Fee-for-Service only. Providers may have additional non-Medicare volume not reflected here. Records with ≤10 beneficiaries are redacted for privacy. First search may take a few seconds to connect.
